Process

We support the founding teams of growing social enterprises by connecting them with product managers from leading technology companies. In rapid sprints, they work together to re-invent the business. We look for passionate Founders, disciplined in use of measurement and eager to use technology to grow their impact.

Product

Our Product program supports enterprises in realising product-led growth, building great experiences by embedding user-centric considerations and capitalising on opportunities to expand the product portfolio.

Seeing your program, service or digital presence as a product allows you to tap into organisational patterns developed over decades in Silicon Valley.

  • What process could maximize team velocity?
  • How to develop a coherent and responsive product strategy?
  • How to embed Human Centric Design practices?
  • What is the right Agile team and how do we fill these roles?

Analytics

Our Analytics program is designed to identify novel analytics applications and manage their implementation across technology, model architecture, data, statistical investigation and other aspects.

An intelligent use of data and analytics can maximise return on funding and support entirely new social business models.

  • How to execute analytical models (hardware, software and tooling)?
  • Which analytical models should we exploit?
  • How to manage and protect our data?
  • When do we need AI vs. business intelligence and what roles fit the challenges we face?

Workflows

Our Workflows program pairs your operators with the best technology to supercharge processes, coordination, speed and service quality.

Your company is a machine. With recent advances in productivity tools and intelligent assistants, the machine has the potential to go a lot faster.

  • What workflow software do we want to use?
  • How do we want our product and business teams to work together?
  • How can we achieve greater oversight our field programs?
  • How can we liberate reporting?

Story

Peteris and Michael met at McKinsey London in 2013 where they saw first-hand how digital was changing the private sector. Now as Product Managers, they started Velocity Program to bring a similar transformation to the social sector. They have a dual mission: using technology to bring measurable impact to the social sector and raising awareness and affinity for social issues in the private sector.

Peteris Erins

Peteris has been working with NGOs on social impact and strategy topics for 4 years via McKinsey and Ashoka. He is a Product Manager at QuantumBlack and previously was a consultant in the EMEA Marketing and Sales Practice at McKinsey. Prior to that he worked as a Software Engineer and Data Scientist at several technology companies including Google and Twitter. He has a BA in Mathematics from Cambridge University.

Michael Cheung

Michael works with NGOs and nonprofits on Analytics and Machine learning infrastructure. He leads Machine Learning investments at a venture capital fund. Prior to this he was a Global Program Director at Tencent, an Analytics Engagement Manager at McKinsey and a Data Scientist at dunnhumby. He has a BEng in Engineering from The University of Warwick
